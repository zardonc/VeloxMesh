<threat_model>
STRIDE register:
- Denial of service: invalid ONNX artifacts must not produce partial runtime behavior. Disposition: fail startup clearly in ONNX mode.
- Tampering: artifact contents must match manifest checksum and supported schema. Disposition: checksum and schema validation before service registration.
- Information disclosure: scheduler responses expose only score, predicted latency, confidence, scheduler version, and reason through the existing internal scheduler contract. Disposition: no proto expansion for raw output-token details unless proven necessary.
- Spoofing/misconfiguration: Phase 15 must not silently switch between heuristic and ONNX. Disposition: explicit service mode and endpoint-selected backend only.
</threat_model>
