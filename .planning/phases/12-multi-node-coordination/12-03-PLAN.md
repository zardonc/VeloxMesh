<threat_model>
STRIDE register:
- Spoofing: Internal topology route must require existing admin identity, not a client-supplied header alone. Disposition: mitigate through middleware.
- Tampering: Consumer apply uses typed ChangeEvent validation before local SQLite mutation. Disposition: mitigate in consumer.
- Repudiation: Failed replay records remain in fallback_log with final failed status. Disposition: mitigate in RecoveryWorker.
- Information disclosure: Ordinary /healthz, /readyz, and data-plane errors must not reveal topology. Disposition: mitigate with integration forbidden-term tests.
- Denial of service: Bounded retry prevents infinite replay loops. Disposition: mitigate in RecoveryWorker.
</threat_model>
